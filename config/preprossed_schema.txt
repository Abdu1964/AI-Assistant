Note
- Transcription factors have label `gene`
- tfbs means transcription factor binding site
- anatomy = UBERON; tissue = BTO; cell_type = CL; cell_line = CLO; experimental_factor = EFO
- biological_process / molecular_function / cellular_component = GO (by subontology)
- disease = Disease Ontology/OMIM/ORPHA; phenotype = HPO; small_molecule = ChEBI
